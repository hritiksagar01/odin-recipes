Samosa

MAGGI Noodles is always a constant companion. Enjoy Cheese MAGGI Noodles, Paneer MAGGI Noodles, Egg MAGGI Noodles, Veg MAGGI Noodles that add a twist of flavour and yumminess to your everyday MAGGI Noodles.

Ingredients

  • MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
    • Oil
  • Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • Tomato (Finely Chopped)
  • Green Chillies (Finely Chopped)
  • Egg (Beaten)

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.

  2. Heat the oil and sauté the onions until they are soft and light brown. Add tomato and green chillies. Sauté, till the tomato has softened.
  3. Cook the MAGGI Masala Noodles as per pack instructions and add to the Egg Bhurji preparation. Mix well.
  4. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
